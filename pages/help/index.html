MintPool Help

Frequently Asked Questions

What is profit-switching?

A profit-switching pool is a virtual network of mining rigs that automatically mine the most profitable coin.

What is profitability?

Profitability is the ratio of how much you can potentially earn by mining the specified coin rather than mining Bitcoin. e.g., a profitability of 1.2 means that the coin is currently 20% more profitable to mine than Bitcoin. This assumes that you immediately take the coin and trade it in for Bitcoins.

What is Scrypt?

Scrypt is the name of the algorithm used by some coins as a proof-of-work. By design, this algorithm is hard to predict, and the lucky miner who predicts a random scrypt hash earns a reward. It is the goal of this pool to successfully solve Scrypt hashes.

What is a proof-of-work?

Simply put, proof-of-work is a puzzle that miners are rewarded for solving. They are typically difficult to solve and the payouts for solving them are unique to each coin.

What are pool fees?

Pool fees are the price miners pay to be part of a pool. The fees pay for server upkeep and employee payroll. The pool fee for MintPool is a low 1.5%

What is Dogecoin?

A crypto-currency based off of an internet meme of a Shiba Inu. You can learn more about it on the Dogecoin subreddit.
